Alpha Shield

Alpha Shield

Durability for real life. Protection for a lifetime.

Our exclusive AlphaShield™ surface treatment delivers an ultra-durable layer of protection that guards against everyday wear and tear, confidently backed by a Lifetime Stain Resistance Guarantee.

AlphaShield™ is scientifically formulated to repel stains such as red wine, coffee, and acidic foods and most household chemicals – with built-in microbial protection for optimal hygiene and food safety assurance.

Nano Enhancement of Surfaces (NEOS) technology creates an impenetrable barrier that’s invisible and permanent, and free from VOCs, fluorine, and halogens.

The AlphaShield™ creation applied during the final stage of manufacture, is embedded into the top layer of the surface, filling the microscopic pores. This process results in Alpha Surfaces’ industry-leading durability and easy-care qualities, as well as enhanced ease of maintenance and surface longevity.
