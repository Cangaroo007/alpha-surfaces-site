Our Story
We built Alpha Surfaces because the industry needed a surface that was as beautiful as it was responsible.

Belinda Kelaher — Managing Director

Alpha Surfaces was founded on a simple belief: premium design and human safety should never be mutually exclusive. Our mineral stone surfaces contain zero crystalline silica, eliminating silicosis risk for every fabricator and installer in the supply chain. Backed by our AlphaShield™ Lifetime Stain Resistance Guarantee, Alpha is the surface your clients will love for decades.
