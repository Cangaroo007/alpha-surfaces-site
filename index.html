About Alpha

A premium Australian benchtop brand built on innovation

Alpha Surfaces is built on a solid foundation of leading innovation and a wealth of industry expertise. Cutting-edge technology delivers mineral stone surfaces of leading durability and practicality, with unmatched natural-stone realism.

As pioneers of silica-free surface technology, we've set a new industry benchmark while delivering the quality and consistency trusted by architects, designers, builders, and fabricators nationwide.
